B'raht Nunh

He was once foolish enough to throw the harp into a campfire. The pain he shared with it was more than enough to learn his lesson.

Crescendo of Chaos

As far as scholars have been able to discern, the magic imbued within the instrument seems to be of Ascian origin. The frame of the harp is unremarkable; carved from fine oak and strung with steel. Arcanists typically sense the presence of something sinister living within it.

Tortuous Tempo

The harp's magic seems to bring out the worst in people. Depending on what Br'aht plays, its chaotic effects range broadly from heavy bouts of melancholy to irresistible inspirations of foolishness. Anybody with a disciplined mind or heightened will seem to suffer little to even no immediate effects. Almost anybody who hears his music is prone to a night of horrific dreams when they next go to sleep.

Hunter's Harmony

B'raht's talents as a musician are equal only to his abilities with a bow. Despite his pacifistic nature, he is an excellent marksman. Local hunters are likely to have heard about or even seen the crimson bard in their travels.

Obscure Opus

Many years travelling with the harp has fractured his perception of reality. B'raht is prone to thick fogs of memory loss. Often vaguely disconnecting in conversation or suffering distracting hallucinations. This inconvenience hampers any long-term relationship, though all this does not seem to faze him in the slightest.

"I don't like large crowds. Things tend to get... out of hand..."

Silandra Crowe

During the Final Days, she sat with the village children inside a nearby cave. The songs she sang drowned out the screams of those suffering outside.

The Mind

She dedicates herself and her passion to the culinary arts. Mostly known for her published cook book, she's always happy to discuss recipes and cooking methods with anybody who recognises her.

The Heart

Silandra is a fountain of empathy towards anybody who finds themselves injured or in trouble. She cares deeply for her friends and welcomes new faces with warmth and hospitality and offers kind and wise words to those in emotional distress.

The Body

Since she was a little girl, she has demonstrated a natural proclivity for art of healing. Although she classifies herself as a strategist, she is more than capable of sustaining her allies in combat. Her optimism, level-headedness and charm naturally declare her a beacon of hope in battle.

The Soul

Her parents were among the Ala Mhigan Resistance. Her upbringing forged her soul, tempered her virtues and sharpened her courage. She takes great pride in her homeland and visits her doting parents frequently.

"If it's one thing everyone can agree on, it's a home cooked meal."

Arlo Briar

Every night, he wakes in silent agony. A glass of whiskey sends him back to his slumber. Another wonderful dream forgotten.

Dolor Qui Absconditus Est

His aversion towards other people is not due to poor manners. Arlo suffers from Osteogenesis Imperfecta. His bones fracture frightfully easily which has resulted in extreme social caution and a general dislike towards bodily contact. He tends to appreciate fellow wallflowers when he's out in public. He welcomes advice on treatments for his peculiar and debilitating disorder.

Libri Et Secreta

Books and literature are a favourite conversation topic for Arlo. A grimoire gifted to him by an old mentor has shaped him to be an accomplished medic and mortician. He's quite unlikely to pass up the chance to visit libraries or new book stores when he's given the chance.

Magna Parva Pera

The small bag he carries rumbles with a muffled noise that is quite disproportionate to its size. It seems to have been imbued with featherlight and expansive properties. It can carry immense objects with ease. He is known to usually carry potions, antidotes, bedrolls, chocobo snacks and even a comprehensive tent and cooking set.

Bona Voluntate

Owing to his lack of social prowess, Arlo has an unfortunate habit of speaking embarrassing truths to people. His flair for awkward questions and remarks is done out of either pure curiosity or concern and means no offence.

"I just wish people were easier to read than books."

The Matron

She would much sooner go to the grave before she tells anybody her deepest secret. It is true that there is indeed power in a name.

Beneath the Masque

On the surface, she can easily be described as nothing more than condescending, impatient, stern, materialistic, arrogant, shrewd and even downright unpleasant. On the contrary, she is known to be quite charitable to societies as well as individuals. There are many in the La Noscea who owe their successful livelihoods thanks to her person intervention.

Behind the Alias

The Matron deems her discretion as a necessary evil and tends not to elaborate further when questioned about her marriage. Gossip in the city streets seem to suggest she adopted the name after hosting a party that orchestrated the scandals of each and every single one of her guests after discovering their attempts to ruin her.

A Lone Fortune

Although it's obvious that she enjoys a life of opulence, she merely markets herself as a simple landlady to those who are playfully brave enough to enquire. To the doubtful, she expresses her enthusiasm for making small personal investments and business acquisitions. There are very few people who are wise to the true origins of her wealth.

The Rose's Thorns

Rumour would correctly have it that the Matron coats her fingernails with a fine layer of spider venom. Anyone unfortunate enough to earn her hand across their face is met with an agonizing burning sensation as the venom reactivates into the fresh cuts. Very few can recognise the deadly sweet scent of the venom.

"If it's within this building, I can assure you it very much is my business..."

Zachery Picquery

He is as bold as he is loyal. Do not be fooled. He is not as frail as he seems. Many years in service have tempered his soul and spirit.

Sea Dog

A good portion of his youth was spent sailing across the Five Seas. He thoroughly enjoys exchanging tales of his adventures with anybody he meets.

Thin Temperment

It isn't a difficult task for any man or woman to warrant a duel from the dapper little man. Only the young and the foolish would be likely to underestimate him. There is a rapier hidden within his walking cane and he still knows exactly how to use it.

Game Veteran

As a man of war, Zachery thoroughly enjoys a good challenge. He frequents the Gold Saucer and delights in making reckless bets. He does not play dirty and detests anybody that does. He will call out a cheater without the slightest hesitation.

Fine Tailor

After the death of his beloved wife, Zachery has continued to maintain the running of her little tailoring boutique. He offers high praise to exquisite colours and well-matched attire. He takes full delight when strangers want to renew their wardrobe.

"True civility always lies within the brim of a sturdy and well-tailored hat!"

Sjaro Eruyt

Bring caution with your intentions. This one treads with purpose... He calls upon the stars and has determined the fates of uncountable lives.

Wayfarer

For many years, Sjaro has roamed the world by Word of the Wood. He is tasked as a messenger to the descendants born beyond Golmore Jungle. He wears peculiar winged sandals that allow him to walk for weeks with no exhaustion. The wings seem to flap and behave with some degree of sentience.

Stargazer

When beyond the safety of the jungle, his travels are guided by the stars. Decades of attunement to the celestial bodies has brought him good fortune in his journeys. His deck of tarot cards hang invitingly against his satchel. There is no doubt that he seems proficient at reading signs from somebody's the past and the future. Under the right circumstances, he might be convinced to do so.

Aljara's Tear

His only prized possession is a necklace fashioned from the amber of the Jungle and blessed by every male in his village. It allows him to listen to the voice of the Wood when he returns to Her after long stretches of time. He is extremely protective of it and will immediately abandon any task in the effort to retrieve it, if stolen.

Traditionalist

The clothes he wears immediately identify him as a foreigner in Eorzea. Living on the boundaries and apart from the women of the Jungle, he finds it easier to speak to other men and does not consider those who have abandoned Eruyt Village as true Viera. Sjaro has a high regard for honesty and an adoration for plant life.

"I speak for the Wood to those whose ears are dulled by the world beyond Her bough."

Darzhan Zelasch

He rips the tongues from his fallen enemies. He believes that by doing so, he will silence the cries of those who burned in the Citadel.

Master Gunbreaker

The Tujiwa travelled across Ilsabard offering their services as wandering gunbreakers. They were known for their signature technique of channeling pure aether into their gunblades in lieu of using shells. Darzhan is exceptional with a gunblade in his hands. Although he cannot actively cast most forms of magic, he is quite capable in unarmed combat and proficient with a greatsword.

Aesthete Weaponsmith

Meeting many different kinds of merchants and artisans throughout his life in a travelling clan, Darzhan gained much experience in the art of weaponry. His hands being far too large for the intricacies of armour crafting and tailoring, he instead focused his skill and strength on tempering metal and bending bow limbs. He takes great pride in his craftsmanship, only forging the finest weaponry.

Photophobic

As a cub, Darzhan and his clan bore witness to the Bozja Incident. The light of the blast was so bright, it burned itself into their eyes. As a result, they spent many years adapting to their oversensitivity. Their already heightened hearing and sense of smell helped them overcome the daylight. Darzhan can be blinded easily without his visor. Strong bursts of light are enough to pierce his shut eyes and causes him great pain.

Lost Valiant

Kumoja died before he had passed on their mark. The mark of the one who would lead their legacy far into the future and uphold the vow they took in solidarity of those innocent who lost their lives in the Citadel. He swore his own oath to remain an envoy of the Tujiwa and to share the secrets of his clan's arts and morals to those with the will to learn and aiding those in need.

"I remember watching the birds every sunset. They took that away from me..."
